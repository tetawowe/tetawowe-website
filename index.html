tetawowe atelier is a young and dynamic design firm specialized in creating design narratives; what is thought to be raw materials taken from the site’s existing rain trees, the geometrical shape of a site that is reminiscent of Miro’s artwork, the way the owners would spend their lazy afternoon, birds on the wire, dialogues between the river and passers-by, a Beatles song, a banal job site depicted in a Kafkan dreamlike space – has been the source of inspiration for our design.
